SharePoint Content Management

If you’ve ever used SharePoint, you know that it is very easy to add content to it. But how exactly should it be organized? And how do you organize it once the content is already in SharePoint?

We can help with SharePoint content management on a variety of levels. Whether you need permissions help, using metadata tagging versus folders or even organizing your sites, we’ve got you covered. We leverage our team of professional SharePoint experts to review your current and future states, come up with a scalable plan and rapidly execute in your environment.

Have you ever uploaded dozens of files only to find that they’re not in any particular order? We’ve seen that happen hundreds of times. In other cases, people might find that they’ve left spaces or extended Unicode characters in numerous file names and are now getting weird URLs as a result

Renaming every single one of these documents might seem like a daunting task, but it’s yet another area where our service can help out.

DID YOU KNOW SHAREPOINT…

Can manage permissions at a very broad as well as very granular level, down to a specific file level.

Has content auditing abilities to meet audit and compliance standards.

Has robust retention policies to help keep your environment clean and healthy.

Has numerous ways to set up and present information ranging from very large, dedicated containers down to individual website pages to lightly present information to users.

Can manage & use file templates such as project plans, invoices or other files that you fill out often during the normal course of business.
