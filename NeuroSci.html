Sense Of Touch Skematic

Presenting our groundbreaking project that redefines the sense of touch in prosthetics. In this innovative design, pressure sensors integrated into the fingers of the prosthetic enable a remarkable range of tactile experiences, from gentle touches to firm grips. Through sophisticated neural communication orchestrated by the Arduino UNO, the brain translates these pressure values into precise commands sent to servos, recreating the sensation on corresponding areas of the body. This pioneering technology creates a seamless fusion between artificial and natural touch, making you feel like the nerves for your fingers reside where the servos are placed. "NeuroTouch Prosthetic: Empowering Minds, Elevating Lives"

Introducing our groundbreaking project: a cost-effective, mind-controlled prosthetic with an extraordinary sense of touch. This innovation aims to transform the lives of paralyzed and amputated individuals, bridging the gap between expensive advanced prosthetics and affordable alternatives. Our pioneering approach incorporates cutting-edge EEG technology, exploring the intricate realms of brain rhythms and motor imagery. By harnessing acquired EEG signals and processing them through our state-of-the-art classifying pipeline, we achieve remarkable precision in deciphering right and left commands. The prosthetic seamlessly responds, closing the hand for left signals and opening it for right signals. What sets us apart is our revolutionary sense of touch, replicating any force experienced on the prosthetic fingers onto a corresponding nerve in the human body. Through meticulous research, we have identified the optimal electrode placement for motor imagery, utilizing eight channels around C3 and C4 points. Our results showcase the successful fusion of EEG-based classification, mind-controlled prosthetics, and a truly immersive sense of touch. This study provides compelling evidence of the presence of in-depth brain rhythms and motor imagery within brain wavelengths. Our project demonstrates the tremendous potential for achieving accurate classification, as well as unlocking the realm of tactile perception. Prepare to be captivated by the limitless possibilities of our mind-controlled prosthetic, where science meets compassion to redefine human potential.

Mind-Controlled Prosthetic
Future Plans

Following the success of the mind-controlled prosthetic, developed by Rishabh Kumar. The development of Mind-Controlled Prosthetics V2 has started, and the new variation of the prosthetic will include various upgrades which will enhance the capabilities of the Mind-Controlled Prosthetic.

  • Future plans for the prosthetic will significantly enhance the capabilities and esthetic making the V2 ready for the industrial market.
  • Prosthetic V2 will replace the bulky headset with a sleek, modern cap hiding the electrode configuration.
  • Prosthetic V2 will have a more significant number of electrodes which will be used to accomplish SSVEP, and enhance the accuracy of prosthetic.

    • Detection of Mental Speech

    One of the most significant upgrades to the Mind-Controlled Prosthetic V2 will be the detection of mental speech. Simply detecting what an individual wants to say, then the prosthetic will be able to speak for them.

    How will the Detection of the Mental Speech system work?

  • With the new electrodes placed near the eyes in the 10-20 verified research electrode placement.
  • A VR head set will be used when using the Detection of Mental SPeech, the VR will be displaying a selecting system of words or alphabet flashing at different frequencies when the user concentrates on a word or letter on the screen the flashing frequency will be detected by the electrodes. The flashing frequency number is sent to the mother board which determines which word the flashing frequency number matches with. After the word is classified it is set to another microcontroller which pronounces the word with the use of small speakers build in the prosthetic. The word will also be displayed on the on board screen from prosthetic V1.
  • This feature is very helpful to the disabled demographic we are targeting for this prosthetic.
  • With the use of Mind-Controlled Prosthetic V2 disabled indivigulls unable to talk and/or amputies will be able to control a prosthetic fully mentally while also talking entirly mentally.
